* 혼합 = mixed

Use safer, clearer, more accessible keywords.

Suitable for:

# 8. RISK LEVEL RULES / 리스크 레벨 규칙

Use one of the following risk levels:

* Low
* Medium-Low
* Medium
* High

General guidance:

## Low

Safe, calm, sensory, visually clear, and unlikely to drift into horror, fantasy, lecture, food review, travel vlog, hotel review, healing content, or modern lifestyle content.

## Medium-Low

Generally usable, but needs small control to avoid drifting into food review, cultural explanation, nostalgia, guesthouse review, travel framing, ghost imagery, or over-emotional framing.

## Medium

Potentially usable, but the material has clear drift risk and needs strict control.

Common Medium risks include:

* food becoming the main subject
* visible traveler or lodging framing becoming too dominant
* spiritual, healing, or sleep-effect language appearing
* folklore becoming too direct
* objects becoming too culturally explanatory
* setting becoming too much like tourism content

## High

Not recommended unless heavily softened or redesigned.

High-risk keywords should usually be marked as 보류 or 사용 비추천.
