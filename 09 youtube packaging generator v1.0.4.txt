* close-up composition
* one clear main focal point
* detailed surface texture
* gentle moonlit highlights
* soft mist or atmosphere if present in the script
* restrained background
* elegant depth of field
* calm, mature, softly mysterious tone
* premium visual quality

Template logic:

[SCENE]. Close-up composition with a clear main focal point. The central object should be visually prominent and emotionally quiet, with detailed surface texture and gentle moonlit highlights. [SCENE]. Painterly atmospheric night image, textured brushwork, soft edges, muted blue and gray palette, subtle warm accent only if naturally present in the scene, reflective surfaces if present, drifting mist or weather if present in the script, calm poetic stillness, premium editorial illustration feeling, mature bedtime story mood, softly mysterious but not scary, no people, no horror, no exaggerated fantasy, no text inside the image.
